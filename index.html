Bengal Tiger

Since tigers hunt mostly at dusk and dawn their stripes help them hide in the shadows of tall grasses.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Tigris
  • Average Length: 111 to 120 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Tropical jungle, brush, marsh lands, and tall grasslands

The bengal tiger ranks among the biggest wildcats alive today. They are carnivores and prefer hunting large ungulates such as guar, sambar and chital. They typically lead solitary lives and only congregate temporarily in special conditions.
